About Us

Built for every kind of fighter

Armour Boxing Fight Club was founded with a vision to make boxing and combat fitness accessible to everyone — students, working professionals, beginners and competitive athletes alike.

We focus on helping individuals develop physical fitness, mental toughness, discipline and confidence through structured training programs, guided by coaches who know the sport from the inside.

Coaching Expertise
  • Boxing Training
  • Taekwondo Training
  • Strength & Conditioning
  • Fitness Coaching
  • Mobility & Injury Prevention
Our Mission

To build a strong community where people of all ages improve their health, fitness and self-confidence through boxing and combat sports.

Our Programs

Five rounds of training

Every program is a round in your training week — pick where you start, the coaches build the rest around you.

Round 01

Boxing Fundamentals

  • Stance
  • Guard
  • Footwork
  • Punching Techniques
  • Defensive Skills
  • Combination Drills
Perfect for beginners and intermediate athletes.
Round 02

Fitness Boxing

  • Weight Loss
  • Fat Burning
  • Cardiovascular Fitness
  • Endurance Improvement
No fighting experience required.
Round 03

Self Defence Training

  • Practical defence skills
  • Situational awareness
  • Confidence building
  • Reaction time
Suitable for men and women.

  • Strength

  • Speed
  • Agility
  • Athletic Performance

Taekwondo Training

  • Discipline
